Born Free?

BORN FREE?

Freedom Has A Price.

"What does Freedom really mean?"

A short film about Freedom, Historical injustice, and Resistance.

Synopsis

A young woman is drawn into a symbolic Art Realm populated by ancestral figures, enslaved labourers, displaced families, and matric students.

In this realm, she is forced to interrogate the true meaning of "Freedom" in a democratic South Africa.

Director's Statement

Born Free? examines the true meaning of liberation and what it means to be truly free in a democratic South Africa.

This film emphasises the disparity between "political freedom" and "economic freedom."

Most importantly, Born Free? is asking, "Was Freedom truly achieved, or is it still an ongoing struggle?"
